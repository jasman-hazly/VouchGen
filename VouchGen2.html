VouchGen User Manual

Version 2.0

VouchGen is a web-based tool designed to automatically parse bulk e-voucher PDFs (Touch 'n Go, Shopee, Lazada, Zus Coffee) and convert them into individual, easy-to-scan coupon cards.

1. Getting Started

Access the tool via your web browser. No installation is required; the tool runs entirely in your browser.

2. Core Features & How to Use

A. Uploading Voucher Data

  1. Click the "Upload" button in the top right corner.
  2. Select the PDF file containing your voucher list (e.g., Touch n Go eWallet E-Gift Vou.pdf).
  3. The tool will process the file. A success message will appear showing how many coupons were loaded.

B. Selecting the Brand

Locate the dropdown menu in the top navigation bar to switch branding (Touch 'n Go, Shopee, Lazada, or Zus Coffee). This updates the logos and colors.

C. Organizing Coupons

  • Group by Value: Toggle the "Group" switch at the top.
    • ON: Coupons are separated into sections (e.g., "MYR 10 Vouchers").
    • OFF: Coupons are listed in a flat grid.

D. Distributing & Redeeming

  • To Send via Email: Click "Redeem", enter recipient email, and choose "Open in Gmail" or "Use default mail app".
  • Manual Redemption: Click "Redeem", leave email blank, and click the link to mark as used.
  • Undo: Click the gray "Redeemed (Undo?)" button and confirm to restore the voucher.

E. Copying PIN Codes

Click the small Copy Icon next to the PIN number on any card to instantly copy the 10-digit PIN.

F. Saving as PDF

Click the "PDF" button to download a printable version. The layout is optimized to fit 4 coupons per A4 page.

3. Troubleshooting

Issue Solution
Coupons not found Ensure you are uploading the original PDF received from the vendor.
Email app doesn't open Use the "Copy Message Body" button in the redeem window and paste it manually.
PINs missing zeros The tool automatically detects short PINs and adds leading zeros (10 digits).

Privacy Note: This tool processes data locally in your browser. Your voucher codes are not uploaded to any external server.
